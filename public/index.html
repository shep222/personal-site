About Me

Where I came from and what I'm doing now

I was born in Mississippi and raised in Alabama. Right out of high school, I joined the US Navy where is served on two different aircraft carriers. After fullfilling my obligation to the Navy, I moved to Paris, TN where I picked up odd jobs while performing as a bullrider on the International Pro Rodeo Association tour. In 2005, I had a calling to serve back in the military as a paratrooper with the US Army. After my service to the country, I relocated back to the gulf coast to the small town of Ocean Springs, MS to run a family owned bar and grill named Shep's Fort Bayou Bar and Grill. During my time running the family business, I used my GI Bill to graduate the University of South Alabama with a Bachelors of Science in Information Systems. With a love of snowboarding and Denver being such a great place for tech, I decided to move to Denver in December of 2013. My first software job was working in CRM in an outdated language. In 2017, with my new passsion for software, I decided to fast track my learning by attending the Full Stack Web Development course at Galvanize.

Hobbies and Interests

I enjoy spending time with my two rescue dogs, Bacchus and Rowdy. I am also an avid tennis player and golfer and enjoy hitting the slopes during the winter months. I'm a dedicated Cubs fan but really enjoy baseball as a whole. I grew up spending most of my time hunting and fishing and have taken up flyfishing since I moved to Colorado.
